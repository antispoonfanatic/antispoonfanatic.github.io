About Me

Hi!
I am Allan Raj and I enjoy teaching and designing learning content. I am currently working as an Assistant Professor of English in Seshadripuram College, Bengaluru. I like to design things on Illustrator and InDesign, paint watercolours, play videogames, and procrastinate—I do not know how I ended up launching this website!

I did my B.A. in Psychology, Journalism and Optional English from Seshadripuram College and my M.A. in English from St. Joseph's College. My Pre-University studies was from Jindal PU College (back then it was co-ed) in Science, with Computer Science as my optional subject (Biology made me squeamish). The HTML I learnt there helped me write this website! I learnt CSS during the 2021 lockdown.

I am also the editor of the alumni magazine by The Seshadripuram College Old Students' Association ® — The Seshadripuram Monthly. You can find the copies here.
