The Kelmac Group® Values

Reflecting on the growth and development of the Kelmac Group®, our success has been grounded in our focus on innovation, assurance, integrity, and investment in the advancement of our staff. In turn, our people and our strategic partners take great pride in embracing and internalizing our core values as we strive to grow professionally and individually.

People

At the Kelmac Group®, we are proud to be a family-owned business with a culturally diverse and inclusive team of exceptional people. We operate a non-hierarchial organization structure, and we openly encourage people engagement and diverging perspectives.

Our teams comprise individuals from around the world, bringing together a range of unique talents, experiences, perspectives, and beliefs. We believe that this diversity is a key driver of innovation, creativity, and teamwork that enables us to approach challenges from multiple angles. We believe that diversity and commitment drive our passion for delivering outstanding and tangible results for our learners and clients.

As a practice, we care for our people, maintain and sustain a caring meritocracy, and embrace different perspectives with curiosity and respect. We look out for each other and support each other like a family.We invest in the development of our people We invest in the development of our people through education, apprenticeship, tools, and mentoring, and we celebrate their achievements. The Kelmac Group® supports the roles that each person plays within their own family and community and strives to achieve a meaningful balance between work, family, community, and self.

Independence

Independence is highly valued at the Kelmac Group®, both within our teams and in how we operate as a practice. We foster an environment that encourages autonomy , empowering our individuals and teams to make independent decisions and take ownership of their work.

We believe in self-reliance and strive to build a culture where we develop our products, services, and digital product platform in-house, from our comprehensive curriculum to our innovative product platform, tools, and resources.

Independence is also a hallmark of our practice. When we deliver our ISO management consulting service, we allow our clients to select their Certification Body or Registrar without our influence. As a policy, we do not attend the ISO certification audits. We want the ISO Certification Body or Registrar to build an independent relationship with each client. When we conduct management system and regulatory audits, we fully embrace the audit principle of independent and impartiality throughout the audit life cycle.

Our independence allows us to maintain control over the quality and innovation of our products, services, and process, including the customization of our offerings, ensuring they meet the unique needs of our clients. By valuing independence, we strive to be a self-sufficient and agile organization, consistently delivering high-quality solutions that set us apart.

Innovation

Since our inception, innovation has been at the heart of everything we do within the Kelmac Group®. We believe passionately in challenging ourselves to find the next breakthrough, to bring ever greater value to our clients. We are committed to fostering an unrivalled environment for exceptional people to stretch their wings.

We can proudly claim we are innovators in the ISO conformity, conformity assessment and regulatory industry sector. We are always pushing boundaries, creating new standards of excellence and sustainability and value for our clients.

Here are some examples:

  • We offer the widest learning curriculum of any independent and Accredited ISO learning organization
  • Our ISO 9001 Fundamentals course was the first to become certified by CQI-IRCA for self-paced/online learning
  • We are successfully transitioning to an AI-based digital platform to enhance the learner journey and learner experience from registration through post-delivery.

Integrity

At the Kelmac Group®, integrity is at the very core of who we are. It shapes every action and decision, allowing us to build lasting trust with our clients, employees, and partners. With a strong moral compass, we prioritize honesty, transparency, and fairness in all our interactions. When challenges arise, we face them head-on, always keeping our clients' best interests in mind. Our team members are empowered to make decisions aligned with our values, fostering a culture of ownership and commitment. Our dedication to integrity is the driving force behind our success and the strong relationships we build with our clients and stakeholders. We have created an unrivaled environment for exceptional people.

Our mission is to create a great practice that attracts, develops, excites, and retains exceptional people. We are also a values-driven organization. Our values reflect the thinking of our founder, Gerard Kelly, who is a major force in shaping the practice to this day. Our values have undergone some minor changes to reflect changing times. They inform both our long-term strategy as a practice and how we serve our clients daily. We set aside time throughout each year to ensure we prioritize and focus on our values. We invest significantly in our people, strategic partners, and strategic alliances to ensure our core values, especially integrity, are embedded in everything we do and what we standard for as a leading ISO conformity, conformity assessment, and regulatory practice.

Trust

Trust is a value that underpins every aspect of the company—amongst our clients or within our teams. We believe in open communication, ensuring that our clients and stakeholders are well-informed and have a clear understanding of our services, processes, and policies. At the Kelmac Group®, we prioritize building and nurturing trust with our stakeholders. By fostering a culture of trust, we create long-lasting relationships and a reputation as a reliable and trustworthy partner.

Client Focus

At the Kelmac Group®, we prioritize our clients above all else. By putting their needs first, we build strong and enduring relationships. Our client-centric approach is rooted in active listening and effective communication. Our consultants take the time to truly understand their requirements, challenges, and goals, allowing us to tailor our offerings to their specific needs. We go beyond the basics, providing valuable insights, industry trends, and best practices. We push our clients not just to do what needs to be done to clear an audit but also to establish a system that will help them be more efficient and organized. This impetus enables them to have a strong management system in place by helping them explore untapped potential. We foster a culture of continuous improvement, offering resources and training opportunities to enhance their knowledge and skills. With us, clients embrace change, learning, and innovation, striving for excellence every step of the way.
